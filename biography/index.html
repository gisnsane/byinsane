BIOGRAPHY

Guillermo Mora is a photographer and visual artist based in Lima. His work combines documentary, conceptual, and experimental approaches, using photography, archival material, and text as narrative tools. He focuses on themes such as identity, memory, and the relationship between the personal and the collective, with particular attention to territory and everyday life. He has been selected as Photographer of the Week by Fisheye (Mexico), participated in the Photopolis Home-S group exhibition in Greece, was a finalist in the Kuala Lumpur International Photo Awards, and was part of the Poemas de la calle (Poems of the Street) exhibition organized by Ruido and Street Photography Peru.
